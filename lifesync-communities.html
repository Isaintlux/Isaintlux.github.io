Complaint Workflow

From Resident to Resolution

A streamlined digital process that keeps residents informed every step of the way.

1

Resident Files Complaint

Submit via web form, mobile app, or in-person kiosk. Photo evidence supported.

2

Auto-Acknowledged

Resident receives instant confirmation with a tracking number via SMS or email.

3

Assigned & Reviewed

Officials review the complaint and assign it to the correct department or officer.

4

DTI Report Generated

Resolved cases are automatically compiled into DTI-ready reports for submission.

Complaint Submission Portal

Residents submit complaints online with category tagging, photo uploads, and location data.
